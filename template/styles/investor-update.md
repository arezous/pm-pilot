# Communication Style: Investor Update

## Format Rules

- **Length:** 300-500 words (investors skim, respect their time)
- **Frequency:** Monthly or quarterly
- **Structure:**
  - Headline metric or milestone (the thing they'll remember)
  - Progress (what moved since last update)
  - Challenges (be honest, they respect transparency)
  - Asks (what you need from them)
- **Metrics:** Lead with the number, then context
- **Tone:** Confident but honest. No spin. Bad news delivered directly.
- **Voice:** Founder voice, not corporate. Short sentences. First person.

## Template

**[SUBJECT: Company Name — Month YYYY Update]**

**Headline:**
[One sentence: the most important thing that happened. Lead with a number if you have one.]

**Progress:**
- [Key metric 1: what it is now, what it was, direction]
- [Key metric 2]
- [Milestone or shipped feature with impact]
- [Customer or market signal worth noting]

**Challenges:**
- [What's not working and what you're doing about it]
- [Where you're stuck or uncertain]

**Asks:**
- [Specific intros, advice, or help needed]

**Next month:**
- [Top 1-2 priorities]

## Example

**Subject: Acme — March 2026 Update**

**Headline:**
MRR crossed $50K for the first time. Up 22% from February.

**Progress:**
- MRR: $51.2K (was $42K in Feb). Growth driven by 3 new mid-market accounts.
- Activation rate: 52% (was 45%). Template library shipped, early signal it's working.
- Closed pilot with [Company X], 200-seat account. Converting to paid in April.
- Featured in ProductHunt newsletter, drove 400 signups in one week.

**Challenges:**
- Churn ticked up to 4.8% (was 3.2%). Investigating — looks like SMB segment, not mid-market. May need to tighten ICP.
- Engineering hiring is slow. Two offers declined. Adjusting comp bands.

**Asks:**
- Intros to mid-market PM leaders (our best-converting segment right now)
- Anyone know strong senior engineers open to early-stage?

**Next month:**
- Ship onboarding redesign (targeting 60% activation)
- Close 2 more mid-market pilots

## When to Use

- Monthly investor updates
- Quarterly board summaries
- Fundraising progress updates
- Milestone announcements to backers
